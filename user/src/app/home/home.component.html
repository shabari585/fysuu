Concept

Are you bored of the monotonous work at your desk? Are you hungry as well? We make your lunch time fun time as well. Order your lunch and be ready with Pen & Paper !!!

We are introducing a ground-breaking idea to make your lunch-time fun, useful and creative. Bringing back the lost art of letter writings, reinventing the concept of daily writing habits and sharpen your mind.

Have a look !
